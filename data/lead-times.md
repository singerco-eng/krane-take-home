## Risk Tier Classification (from lead time ranges)

These tiers can be used to auto-classify any procurement item based on its CSI division and item type:

| Tier | Lead Time | Action Required | Examples |
|---|---|---|---|
| **CRITICAL** | 40+ weeks | Must order immediately, may need interim solution | Transformers, switchgear, elevators, generators |
| **LONG-LEAD** | 16-40 weeks | Order within first 2 weeks, submittal priority | Structural steel, AHUs, roofing, curtain wall |
| **MODERATE** | 8-16 weeks | Order within first month | Precast, doors, fire suppression, casework |
| **STANDARD** | 4-8 weeks | Order per normal schedule | Plumbing fixtures, rebar, insulation |
| **COMMODITY** | 1-4 weeks | Order as needed | Paint, concrete, lumber, standard fasteners |
